Biography

Aiko was born in Los Angeles. Her family lived in South Los Angeles, but their home burned down when she was seven so they moved near Slauson Avenue around View Park–Windsor Hills, Baldwin Hills, and Ladera Heights where she was homeschooled until high school. She calls her home neighborhood "Slauson Hills". Aiko is the daughter of Christina Yamamoto and Karamo Chilombo, a pediatrician. Her parents are divorced.[15] Her sister is R&B singer Mila J. Her mother is of Spanish, Dominican, and Japanese descent, and her father is of Native American, African-American, and German-Jewish descent. She took vocal lessons in Culver City, California, but quit when she unexpectedly became pregnant. At age 20, she gave birth to a baby girl she named Namiko Love Browner.
